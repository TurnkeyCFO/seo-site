Questions

The honest answers.

How long until I see results?

SEO is a compounding investment, not an overnight switch. Most businesses see early movement in the first 1–3 months as technical fixes land, with meaningful ranking and traffic gains building from there. We set clear expectations up front and report progress every month so you're never guessing.

Do I have to sign a long contract?

No. We don't believe in trapping clients in long agreements. We earn the relationship every month with results you can see. If we're not moving the needle, you shouldn't be locked in.

Will this hurt my site or get me penalized?

Never. Everything we do is white-hat and aligned with Google's guidelines — relevance-first links, real content, clean technical work. We build durable authority, not shortcuts that collapse at the next algorithm update.

What does reporting look like?

Plain English. Each month you get a clear view of rankings, organic traffic, and the leads or actions that actually matter to your business — tied to what we did and what's next. No jargon wall, no vanity dashboards.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

Local service businesses, growth-stage companies, churches, and nonprofits — organizations that want to be found by the people already searching for what they do. If your customers use Google, there's opportunity here.
